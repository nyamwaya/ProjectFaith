Spontanious Evangilism

We gather together for worship every Saturday. We interact with each other, we participate in small groups where we, honestly, share in singing of songs, studying of the Word and in praising the Lord. We at Faith Church International want to encourage you, equip you and then release you into the community to do spontaneous evangelism!

Worship with us

We invite you to come and worship with us every Sabbath Day! At Faith church International our goal is to make your worship experience with us meaningful and experientially relevant.
